Privacy notice

This website uses cookies that are only active while you are on the site. These cookies help make it so you don't have to enter your information more than once as you move around My VAC Account. Your information isn't saved after you leave the site. If you have cookies turned off on your device, you won't be able to use My VAC Account.

If you are using a public or shared device, it's very important to close your browser after you sign out of My VAC Account.

This service is only for the registered user. Using it in a way that is not allowed or not appropriate could result in your access being restricted.

By clicking Continue, you are saying that you have read and understand the Privacy Notice.
