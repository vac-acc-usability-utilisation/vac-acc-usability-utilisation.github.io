Privacy Notice

Veterans Affairs Canada (VAC) takes your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

The personal information you provide through My VAC Account is collected under the authority of the Department of Veterans Affairs Act and Section 6 of the Policy on Communications and Federal Identity. Your information will only be used to verify your identity, provide you access to the online services, and for the administration of benefits and services.

Your personal information is managed based on the Privacy Act. You have the right to access your personal information and to request changes if the information contains errors. If you are unhappy with how we handle your personal information, you can file a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner of Canada. Additional information about how VAC handles your personal information can be found in the Information about Programs and Information Holdings on the Veterans Affairs Canada website. Information about how the Veterans Review and Appeal Board (VRAB) handles your personal information can be found in their Information about Programs and Information Holdings publication.

Session Cookies

This website uses session cookies. Session cookies are enabled as you navigate through My VAC Account so that you do not need to input information more than once. Your data is not saved once your session is complete. If you have cookies turned off on your browser, you will be unable to use My VAC Account.

If you are using a shared computer, we strongly recommend that you close your browser after you sign out of My VAC Account.

This service is intended for use by the registered user only. Inappropriate or unauthorized use of this service could result in restrictions being placed on your online access.
