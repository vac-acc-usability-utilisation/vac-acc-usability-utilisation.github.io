Identify third parties

Why are we asking this?

In order to make a decision about your application, we may request your personal information from third parties you are receiving payment from for your claimed conditions, or other organizations you authorize VAC to contact.

What are some examples of third parties?

Third parties may be:

  • Worker's compensation
  • Third party insurance
  • Other disability benefit providers
  • Medical physician's you saw for any of your claimed conditions
  • Any other organization or person who may hold information that will support your claim
