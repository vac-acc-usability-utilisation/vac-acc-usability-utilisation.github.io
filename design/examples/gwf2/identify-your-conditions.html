Identify your conditions

What if I don't see my condition in the lists?

The lists only include the most common conditions that members and Veterans are applying for. Not all conditions are listed. If you have a condition that is not included, you can enter your condition by selecting "Other" in either list.

What are some examples of Dental condition?

Some examples of Dental condition are:

  • Bruxism
  • Temporomandibular Joint Dysfunction (TMC)

What are some examples of Respiratory condition?

Some examples of Respiratory condition are:

  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
  • Chronic Bronchitis/Emphysema
  • Asthma
  • Obstructive Sleep Apnea

What are some examples of Stomach or bowel condition?

Some examples of Stomach or bowel condition are:

  • Gastroesophageal Reflux (GERD)
  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)
  • Crohns/Colitis

What are some examples of Reproductive condition?
